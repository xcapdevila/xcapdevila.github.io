Xavier Capdevila

Deputy Head of Software Development

Software Engineer with more than 7 years of experience. Java / JEE specialist.

I am currently Deputy Head of Software Development at fonYou where I am part of the development team, where I design and code IT solutions for value added services for telecommunication companies worldwide. In addition, I collaborate with the Head of Software Development in teams and projects management, and I assume his role in his absence.

Prior to joining fonYou, I worked for a small engineering company - QPrima Enginyeria (which merged with TrantorSystem) - where I evolved from a junior programmer to a technical management position. This stage, apart from providing a great technical experience, helped me to acquire a global vision of the projects being able to know what happens in each area and thus develop a great capacity for analysis and decision making at both strategical and technical levels.

I am passionate about my work and, although I enjoy all aspects of it, my favorite stage of a project is, after working with product's unit leader to understand the business objectives, design the IT solution and its architecture. I love learning new technologies, such as programming languages or frameworks, and new design architectures; And I am always evolving through continuous training.

I am a senior technician in multiplatform application development by the Institut Obert de Calaunya (IOC) and I am currently studying for a degree in Computer Engineering at the Universitat Oberta de Catalunya (UOC). In addition I have done several specialization courses like Java Programmer Analyst, Java Multithreading, Android Developer, JavaScript: Understanding the Weird Parts, Learn and Understand AngularJS, among others.

I like to stay in touch with other IT experts. I am open to connecting with former colleagues, managers, or just interesting people, so feel free to contact me if you want to connect.

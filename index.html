In addition I have done several specialization courses like Java Programmer Analyst, Java Multithreading, Android Developer, JavaScript: Understanding the Weird Parts, Learn and Understand AngularJS, among others.

I like to stay in touch with other IT experts. I am open to connecting with former colleagues, managers, or just interesting people, so feel free to contact me if you want to connect.
